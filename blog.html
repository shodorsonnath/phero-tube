Discuss the scope of var, let and const

var, let and const are variable in JavaScript. Here, The var is the oldest keyword to declare a variable and var declarations are globally scoped or function scoped. let is the alternative option or keyword. let variable have the block-level scope. const variable is used to declare that variable cannot be reassigned.
